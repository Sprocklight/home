Clients

Case Study

Beaumont Bakery & Café (Millcreek, Utah) is a beloved local café and bakery known for its artisanal breads, beautiful pastries, and warm, inviting atmosphere. The goal of the redesign was to refine their digital presence—updating photography, banners, and overall UI/UX—to match their elevated in-store experience and make their site feel more premium, clean, and user-friendly.

UI/UX + Visual Refresh

We overhauled the site’s visual assets—upgrading photos, reworking banners, adjusting layout hierarchies—to give Beaumont a more polished and cohesive look. Clean lines, generous whitespace, high-resolution imagery of their signature bakery items (croissants, avocado toast, etc.), and a color palette that reflects warmth but looks upscale were all part of the refresh. The menus, event pages, and order flow were also smoothed out so users feel confident and instinctively guided when navigating.

Banner & Photographic Storytelling

The banners were redesigned to highlight the food and ambiance. Instead of flat or generic imagery, we used styled food photography (pastries, café setting, natural light) that lets the visuals communicate quality and experience. Messaging in the banners was simplified—short, evocative phrases that match the refined feel. These changes help tell the story of Beaumont as not just a place to eat, but a place people want to linger in.

Menu & Ordering UI Improvements

Because Beaumont offers both dine-in and online orders (plus catering), the ordering sections, menus, and event pages needed clarity and easy flow. We cleaned up typography for readability, optimized mobile views, reorganized menu categories so items are easier to find, and made sure “Order Online” and “Catering / Events” options are always obvious. The result is a more intuitive experience for both visitors and staff managing the site.

Visit Beaumont Bakery & Café

I built their website from scratch and designed every interaction—UI/UX, visuals, banners—while also managing backend processes like product sourcing, inventory management, and newsletter strategy to support growth and consistency across all touch points.

UI/UX & Website Build

I crafted custom banners and visual content to elevate product presentation and create a brand look that feels trustworthy and modern.

Product Sourcing & Inventory Management

I handled sourcing of product inventory—ensuring the quality, selection, and pricing align with brand positioning. On the backend, I set up inventory workflows so stock levels are accurate, restocking is timely, and users are not frustrated by “out of stock” surprises.

Newsletter & Customer Engagement

To keep customers connected and coming back, I implemented a newsletter strategy: clean, branded email templates, curated content (new arrivals, promotions, product stories), and seamless opt-in flows integrated with the site. This helped increase repeat visits and build brand loyalty.

Case Study

Beaucoup Bakery (Salt Lake City, Utah) is a French-inspired bakery + market known for fine pastries, specialty breads, and café fare. The owner approached us wanting a full brand refresh—moving away from casual café vibes toward something more elegant, upscale, and polished. They also needed to modernize their website and Toast POS system so the whole customer experience felt high end and consistent.

Branding & Identity Overhaul

We elevated their visual identity with refined typography, a sophisticated color palette, and upscale imagery. The goal was to communicate luxury without losing warmth—so it feels premium but still inviting. Every design element, from logo to packaging, was reworked to give Beaucoup that “boujee” edge.

Website Redesign

The new site emphasizes elegance and ease. Clean layouts, large imagery of pastries & café setting, intuitive navigation, and mobile-first design all play into a refined experience. We pushed for subtle yet effective luxury cues—thoughtful whitespace, high-quality photos, and a cohesive visual style that matches the physical bakery.

Toast POS Integration & UI Enhancements

We updated their Toast POS setup to streamline operations and ensure the POS interface and customer-facing elements align with the new brand identity. That meant cleaning up the POS visuals, optimizing workflows for staff, and ensuring consistency between in-store, online ordering, and branding cues.

SEO Marketing

We first made sure the site content was set up for success by implementing our keywords in meta titles and descriptions, title tags, and in the content on specific target pages.
